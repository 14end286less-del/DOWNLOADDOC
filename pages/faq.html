Questions

Everything you need to know about DownloadDoc.

Is DownloadDoc really free?

Yes. Free forever. No run-time limits, no premium tiers, no watermarks.

Is my data safe?

Totally. Files are processed locally in your browser. We never see or store your documents.

What formats are supported?

Images (PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP) compress natively. PDFs and Word docs are supported with embedded image optimization.

How much will my file shrink?

Images often see 50–80% reduction at good quality. Document results vary. Try the quality slider to balance size vs clarity.

Do I need to create an account?

Nope. No account, no email, no sign-up required. Just open the site and start compressing.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The interface is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Can I compress multiple files at once?

Currently one file at a time. You can click Compress Another after each run.
