Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

DownloadDoc is a browser-based tool. We do not require user accounts, and we do not transmit your files to any server. This policy explains the limited data we store locally in your browser.

What we store locally

We use localStorage to remember your preferences only. This includes:

  • Theme choice — remembers if you use dark or light mode.
  • Cookie consent — remembers whether you accepted or declined the banner.

Third-party cookies

We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking scripts. No cookies are set by third parties when you use DownloadDoc.

Managing your data

You can clear this stored data at any time through your browser settings. Clearing site data will reset your theme and cookie consent preference.
